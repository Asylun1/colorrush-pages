Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Your ball rolls forward automatically. Tap and steer to match your ball's color to the panels and gaps ahead — pass through matching colors and smash the rest. Hitting the wrong color ends your run. Survive as long as you can to climb the score.

Is there any cost to play?

Color Rush is completely free to download and play.

Is my high score saved?

Yes — your high score and preferences are stored locally on your device. Note that because nothing is tied to an account, your score does not transfer between devices and is removed if you delete the app.

The game runs slowly or crashed. What should I do?

Try fully closing and reopening the app, and make sure you're on the latest version from the App Store. If the problem continues, email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll look into it.
