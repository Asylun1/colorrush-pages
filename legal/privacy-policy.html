Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026
The short version: Color Rush does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no logins, and no tracking. Your high score and settings stay on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how Color Rush (the “App,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), developed by asylun, handles information when you use our mobile game. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Color Rush does not ask for, gather, or transmit information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or device identifiers. We do not require you to create an account or sign in to play.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App saves a small amount of information locally on your device only, such as your high score and sound/gameplay preferences. This data:

  • never leaves your device,
  • is not transmitted to us or any third party,
  • and is permanently removed when you delete the App.

3. Third-Party Services

The current version of Color Rush does not include advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking software development kits (SDKs). No data is shared with advertisers, analytics providers, or any external service.

4. Children's Privacy

Color Rush is suitable for all ages and is rated 4+. Because the App does not collect any personal information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will address it promptly — though, as noted, the App is not designed to receive such information.

5. Data Security

Because Color Rush does not transmit or store your data on any server, there is no personal data for us to secure on your behalf. Any locally stored game data is protected by your device's own operating system safeguards.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if a future version of the App introduces new features such as advertising. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above and post the new policy at this address. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
